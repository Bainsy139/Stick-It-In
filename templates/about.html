🚀 About Stick It In V3 – Club Edition 25/26

Stick It In V3 isn’t fantasy football — it’s a strategic prediction league built around digital club ownership and community-driven value creation. Only 20 clubs exist. Each is managed, valued, and—potentially—sold like a digital asset.

🏟️ Built like a business

  • ✅ You run a club — every decision affects your value and ranking.
  • ✅ Prediction performance builds team worth — accuracy = growth.
  • ✅ Community consensus fuels the ecosystem — shared wins = rising pot.
  • ✅ Real buy-out offers at the end of the season based on your club’s value.
  • ✅ Mid-season buyouts optional — listed clubs may receive offers via the public Team Directory.

⚙️ How the economy works

  • Predict 10 results + 10 BTTS calls each week (Mon–Fri).
  • Game Entry closes Friday 23:59. No edits during live match weekends.
  • Points are earned for accuracy — boosting your club’s weight and reputation.
  • A performance-weighted value is calculated weekly — your team is a growing asset.
  • The stronger the community’s collective accuracy, the higher the shared prize pool.
  • 🛒 Action Cards like Red Cards or Injuries can be bought (or earned) to disrupt rivals.
  • 🔻 Miss 3 full weeks and your club enters administration — it will be offered to a new manager.

💰 Value, rewards & legacy

  • 📊 Club value is tracked publicly and updated weekly.
  • 🏆 On 31 May 2026, buy-out offers are made based on team performance.
  • 💼 Mid-season offers are optional — listed clubs can be sold to waiting managers.
  • 🔐 Once sold, your value resets — you step aside for the new owner.
  • 🎯 Top finishers earn the highest returns — but every club has earning potential.

📩 How to claim a club

  • Take the timed football IQ quiz.
  • Score 8/10 or better to join the shortlist.
  • If selected, you’ll be invited to claim and manage a club for the season.
  • First come first served.
  • At the end of the season, you can choose to remain with your club or apply to take over another.

🧠 Why only 20 clubs?

Version 3 is about focus, ownership, and performance. No ghosts. No half-commitments. Just 20 strategic minds competing in a closed economy, where performance builds both status and stake.
