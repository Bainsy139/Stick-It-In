About

What this is

A strategic prediction league built around accuracy, discipline, and long-term value.

Overview

This platform is not a tipster service, betting group, or fantasy league. It is a structured prediction system that aggregates individual decisions over a full football season.

Participants submit weekly predictions within a fixed window. Accuracy is tracked, ranked, and used to determine league position and contribution to the community pot.

How it works

Each game week runs Monday to Friday. Submissions are locked before the weekend fixtures begin. Once locked, predictions cannot be changed.

League position is determined by performance over time, not individual weeks. Consistency is rewarded. Non-participation is penalised.

What it is not

There are no guarantees, shortcuts, or incentives for reckless play. Visibility and reward are reserved for those who participate fully and perform consistently.

The system is automated, rules-based, and impartial. Enforcement is applied quietly and uniformly.

Access

Participation is open. However, only the top tier of the league is eligible for value distribution from the community pot.

Seasonal · Performance-driven · Transparent

This platform operates on a seasonal basis · Rules may evolve between seasons
